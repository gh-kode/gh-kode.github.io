Super Treasure Arena on Steam

About the Game

Super Treasure Arena is a unique mix of retro-inspired arcade action and objective-based arena shooters.

The result is a frantic game where collecting and stealing loot from your opponents is the key to victory.

The action is fast and fun, and with enemy players and monsters all over the place, you'll need to watch your back.

Play locally via split-screen, or challenge other players online!

Enter The Arena

Choose a character and battle it out on tight top-down maps, filled with angry monsters and shiny loot.

Various weapons scattered across each map can be picked up and will help you blasting away your opponents, including bows, magic staffs, bombs, rockets and more!
